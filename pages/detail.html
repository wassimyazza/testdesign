Description

Experience authentic Moroccan hospitality in this beautifully restored riad in the heart of Marrakech's historic medina. This property seamlessly blends traditional Moroccan architecture with modern comfort, featuring intricate tile work, carved wooden ceilings, and a peaceful central courtyard with a refreshing plunge pool. The riad accommodates up to 6 guests across 3 elegantly furnished bedrooms, each with its own unique character. Two spacious lounges provide perfect spaces for relaxation, while the roof terrace offers stunning views of the city skyline and the Atlas Mountains in the distance. Our centrally located property provides easy access to the vibrant souks, historic palaces, and iconic landmarks of Marrakech, while offering a tranquil retreat from the bustling streets.
